This is a customizable widget which will displays the popular posts on your blog on the based on post views.

= Features =
* WordPress widgets for Popular Posts on your blog.
* Display post thumbnail.
* Fully Responsive.
* WordPress Multisite support.

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin and navigate to wp-contents/plugins and extract there.
2. Go to wp-admin/plugins and Activate lwd Popular Posts Widget plugin.
4. Navigate to  Appearance > Widgets, drag the lwd Popular Posts Widget to wherever you want it to be and click on Save.
